Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

VoltLot ("we", "us", or "our") operates the voltlot.com website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when submitting a quote request, including:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Company name (if provided)
  • Product preferences and order details
  • Destination country
  • Any additional requirements you include

We also automatically collect basic technical data such as your IP address and browser type for security and rate-limiting purposes. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Process and respond to your quote requests
  • Communicate with you about your orders and inquiries
  • Protect our website from spam and abuse (rate limiting)

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following cases:

  • Email delivery: We use Resend, a third-party email service, to deliver quote notification emails. Your data is transmitted securely to this provider solely for email delivery.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Retention

Quote request data is transmitted via email and is not stored in a database on our servers. Email records are retained as long as necessary for business operations and legal compliance.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including HTTPS encryption, security headers, and input validation. However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for future communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@voltlot.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Your continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
